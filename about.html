Katie is a...

designer, feminist, homebody

She is currently a Senior Product Designer at Vox. Previously, she was a designer at Sparkbox, Happy Cog, and Hyperquake. She strongly values team collaboration, performance in design, and the unapologetic bond of women.

Offline, she tries to avoid the screen as much as possible. She enjoys her dog, her plants, reading, skincare, hiking, yoga, making candles, and learning to play bass. You'll rarely find her wearing anything other than black.

You can find her doing fun web things like:

  • Winning the Net Awards 2014 Young Designer of the Year
  • Co-hosting The Path to Performance Podcast
  • Redesigning CodePen in the Open
  • Discussing the CodePen Redesign on the RWD Podcast
  • Talking about design on Shoptalk Podcast
  • Battling on Typefight
  • Teaching Girl Scouts how to make websites
  • Teaching Typography and RWD for Girl Develop It
