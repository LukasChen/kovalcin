Kov-al-what?

Katie is a designer in Austin, Texas.

She currently works at Happy Cog. She has a degree in advertising and graphic design from Columbus College of Art & Design where she fell in love with lettering and paper. Her passion for typography and layouts transcended her to shift from print to web design.

When her face isn’t pressed against her computer screen she can be found obsessing over her dog, riding her bike and playing pinball. She can’t resist (another) red lipstick or bad karaoke.

You can find her doing fun things like:

  • Winning the Net Awards 2014 Young Designer of the Year
  • Speaking at CSS Dev Conf
  • Talking about design on Shoptalk Podcast
  • Battling on Typefight
  • Teaching Typography and RWD for Girl Develop It Austin
